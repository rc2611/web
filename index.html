About Me

I am a PhD candidate at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi), specializing in Water Resources Engineering. My research focuses on understanding the complex interactions between atmospheric processes and hydrological extremes.

I completed my B.Tech in Civil Engineering from the National Institute of Technology Hamirpur (NIT Hamirpur) and an M.S. by Research from the Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad (IIT Palakkad).

My current work explores atmospheric moisture transport and its influence on extreme rainfall and flood dynamics using both data-driven and process-based models.
